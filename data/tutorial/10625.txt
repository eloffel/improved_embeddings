efficient structured id136 for transition-based parsing with neural
networks and error states

   ashish vaswani, kenji sagae

abstract

   transition-based approaches based on local classification are
   attractive for id33 due to their simplicity and speed,
   despite producing results slightly below the state-of-the-art. in this
   paper, we propose a new approach for approximate structured id136
   for transition-based parsing that produces scores suitable for global
   scoring using local models. this is accomplished with the introduction
   of error states in local training, which add information about
   incorrect derivation paths typically left out completely in
   locally-trained models. using neural networks for our local
   classifiers, our approach produces the highest accuracy for
   transition-based id33 in english.
