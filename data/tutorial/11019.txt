quadratic id178 and analysis of diversity

     * [11]authors and affiliations

   first online: 17 june 2010
     * 470 downloads

abstract

   in this paper some general postulates are laid down for the
   construction of diversity measures and conditions for anova type of
   analysis are investigated. it is shown that a diversity measure called
   quadratic id178 introduced by the author in 1982, applicable to both
   qualitative and quantitative data, provides a general solution to both
   the problems posed above.
