  course description

   deep learning is a family of methods that exploits using deep
   architectures to learn high-level feature representations from data.
   recently, these methods have helped researchers achieve impressive
   results in various fields within artificial intelligence, such as
   id103, id161, and natural language processing.
   this course provides an overview of deep learning and neural networks;
   the goal is to establish a foundational understanding at a level
   sufficient for students to start reading research papers in this
   exciting and growing area.

   prerequisites: basic calculus, id203, id202.
