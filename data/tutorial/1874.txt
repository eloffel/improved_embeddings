universal dependencies

   universal dependencies (ud) is a framework for cross-linguistically
   consistent grammatical annotation and an open community effort with
   over 200 contributors producing more than 100 treebanks in over 70
   languages.
     * [4]short introduction to ud
     * [5]ud annotation guidelines
     * more information on ud:
          + [6]how to contribute to ud
          + [7]tools for working with ud
          + [8]discussion on ud
          + [9]ud-related events
     * query ud treebanks online:
          + [10]sets treebank search maintained by the university of turku
          + [11]pml tree query maintained by the charles university in
            prague
          + [12]kontext maintained by the charles university in prague
          + [13]grew-match maintained by inria in nancy
          + [14]iness maintained by the university of bergen
     * [15]download ud treebanks

   if you want to receive news about universal dependencies, you can
   subscribe to the [16]ud mailing list. if you want to discuss individual
   annotation questions, use the [17]github issue tracker.
