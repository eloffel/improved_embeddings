berkeley document summarizer

overview

   the berkeley document summarizer is a single-document summarization
   system described in the following paper:

   [7]learning-based single-document summarization with compression and
   anaphoricity constraints
   greg durrett, taylor berg-kirkpatrick, and dan klein. acl 2016

   [8]project page on github.
