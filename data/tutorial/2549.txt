   what this site provides:
    1. [1]introductory essay on these free association norms (260k)
    2. the database (38 mb)
          + [2]appendix a. the normed cues, their targets and related
            information
          + [3]appendix b. have a target word, need a cue or a prime?
          + [4]appendix c. n x n matrices showing network connections
          + [5]appendix d. normed words and their idiosyncratic responses
          + [6]appendix e. accessibility index
          + [7]appendix f. norms for rhyme, assonance, and fragment cues

   how to cite these norms in your references:
   nelson, d. l., mcevoy, c. l., & schreiber, t. a. (1998). the university
   of south florida word association, rhyme, and word fragment norms.
   http://www.usf.edu/freeassociation/.
