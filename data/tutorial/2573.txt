python nltk demos for natural language text processing

   there are currently 4 python nltk demos available. on the top left you
   can do id31, which uses text classification to determine
   sentiment polarity. on the top right, you can see how different word
   tokenizers work. on the bottom left, you can try id30 text in 17
   supported languages. on the bottom right, you can do part of speech
   tagging with 22 different part of speech taggers, along with the
   possibility of chunk extraction and id39. enter
   some text and see what happens!
