                                 practicals

   please click on timetables on the right hand side of this page for time
   and location of the practicals. the instructors are [44]brendan
   shillingford and marcin moczulsky.

   practicals will use [45]torch, a powerful programming framework for
   deep learning that is very popular at google and facebook research.
   practical on week 2: (1) learning lua and the tensor library. [46]pdf
   practical on week 3: (2) online and batch id75. [47]pdf
   practical on week 4: (3) id28 and optimization. [48]pdf
   practical on week 5: continued previous practical.
   practical on week 6: (4) feedforward neural networks, and implementing
   your own layer. [49]pdf
   practical on week 7: (5) intro to nngraph for graph-shaped modules.
   [50]pdf
   practical on week 8: (6) training a lstm language model. [51]pdf
   see the [52]github repository list for the practicals' code and
   technical instructions.
