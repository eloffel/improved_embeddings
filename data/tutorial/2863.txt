readme.md

twitter-text

   this repo is a collection of libraries and conformance tests to
   standardize parsing of tweet text. it synchronizes development,
   testing, creating issues, and pull requests for twitter-text's
   implementations and specification. these libraries are responsible for
   determining the quantity of characters in a tweet and identifying and
   linking any url, @username, #hashtag, or $cashtag.

   see implementations and conformance in this repo below:
