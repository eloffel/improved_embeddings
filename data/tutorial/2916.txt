rrp #1: tom     mikolov on id97 and ai research at microsoft, google,
facebook

   [49]radim   eh    ek 2017-02-09[50] podcast[51] 7 comments

   episode summary: today i sat down with tom     mikolov, my fellow czech
   countryman whom most of you will know through his work on id97. but
   tom     has many more interesting things to say beside id97 (although
   we cover id97 too!): his beginnings with 8bit graphics and games,
   living in ny compared to california, ai research at microsoft vs google
   vs facebook, the importance of focusing on the right thing and tom    '
   vision for the "roadmap towards machine intelligence".

   iframe:
   [52]https://w.soundcloud.com/player/?url=https%3a//api.soundcloud.com/t
   racks/306828573&color=0066cc&auto_play=false&hide_related=false&show_co
   mments=true&show_user=true&show_reposts=false

   links & resources:
    1. tom    ' current affiliation: [53]facebook ai research (fair);
       [54]fair profile, [55]google scholar profile
    2. mikolov, joulin, baroni: a roadmap towards machine intelligence
       [[56]pdf]
    3. a shorter version of that paper that tom     mentioned: baroni &al:
       commai: evaluating the first steps towards a useful general ai
       [[57]pdf + [58]github]
    4. original [59]id97 tool; [60]tutorial for our python port in
       gensim
    5. original [61]fasttext tool; our [62]gensim wrapper for python
