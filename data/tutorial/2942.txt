readme.md

practical neural networks for nlp

   a tutorial given by [70]chris dyer, [71]yoav goldberg, and [72]graham
   neubig at emnlp 2016 in austin. the tutorial covers the basic of neural
   networks for nlp, and how to implement a variety of networks simply and
   efficiently in the [73]dynet toolkit.
     * [74]slides, part 1: basics
          + computation graphs and their construction
          + neural networks in dynet
          + recurrent neural networks
          + minibatching
          + adding new differentiable functions
     * [75]slides, part 2: case studies in nlp
          + tagging with bidirectional id56s and character-based embeddings
          + transition-based id33
          + id170 meets deep learning
