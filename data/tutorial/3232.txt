   every week, new papers on id3 (gan) are
   coming out and it   s hard to keep track of them all, not to mention the
   incredibly creative ways in which researchers are naming these gans!
   you can read more about gans in this [12]generative models post by
   openai or this [13]overview tutorial in kdnuggets.
