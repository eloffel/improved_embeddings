   nltk corpus

   accessing text corpora in nltk is very easily. nltk provides a [34]nltk
   corpus package to read and manage the corpus data. for example, nltk
   includes a small selection of texts from the project gutenberg
   electronic text archive, which name gutenberg corpus. about [35]project
   gutenberg:

     project gutenberg (pg) is a volunteer effort to digitize and archive
     cultural works, to    encourage the creation and distribution of
     ebooks   . it was founded in 1971 by michael s. hart and is the oldest
     digital library. most of the items in its collection are the full
     texts of public domain books. the project tries to make these as
     free as possible, in long-lasting, open formats that can be used on
     almost any computer. as of 3 october 2015, project gutenberg reached
     50,000 items in its collection.

   id97

   narrowly speaking, the id97 we said here is referred to google
   [36]id97 project, which first proposed by [37]tomas mikolov and etc
   in 2013. for more id97 related papers, tutorials, and coding
   examples, we recommend the    [38]getting started with id97    by
   [39]textprocessing.

   id97 in python

   the best python implementation of id97 is gensim id97 module:
   [40]models.id97     deep learning with id97. we have written an
   article about    [41]id97 in python   , you can reference it first if
   you have no idea about gensim id97 model: [42]getting started with
   id97 and glove in python.
