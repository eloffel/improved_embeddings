readme.md

sockeye

   [122]pypi version [123]github license [124]github issues [125]build
   status [126]documentation status

   this package contains the sockeye project, a sequence-to-sequence
   framework for id4 based on apache mxnet
   (incubating). it implements state-of-the-art encoder-decoder
   architectures, such as:
     * deep recurrent neural networks with attention [[127]bahdanau, '14]
     * transformer models with self-attention [[128]vaswani et al, '17]
     * fully convolutional sequence-to-sequence models [[129]gehring et
       al, '17]

   in addition, it provides an experimental [130]image-to-description
   module that can be used for image captioning. recent developments and
   changes are tracked in our [131]changelog.

   if you have any questions or discover problems, please [132]file an
   issue. you can also send questions to sockeye-dev-at-amazon-dot-com.

documentation

   for information on how to use sockeye, please visit [133]our
   documentation. developers may be interested in our [134]developer
   guidelines.

citation

   for technical information about sockeye, see our paper on the arxiv
   ([135]bibtex):

     felix hieber, tobias domhan, michael denkowski, david vilar, artem
     sokolov, ann clifton and matt post. 2017. [136]sockeye: a toolkit
     for id4. arxiv e-prints.
