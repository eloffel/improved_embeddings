readme.md

really-awesome-gan

   a list of papers and other resources on generative adversarial (neural)
   networks. this site is maintained by holger caesar. to complement or
   correct it, please contact me at holger-at-it-caesar.com or visit
   [68]it-caesar.com. also checkout
   [69]really-awesome-semantic-segmentation and our [70]coco-stuff
   dataset.

   note: despite the enormous interest in this cite (~3000 visitors per
   month), i will no longer add new papers starting from november 2017. i
   feel that gans have come from an exotic topic to the mainstream and an
   exhaustive list of all gan papers is no more feasible or useful.
   however, i invite other people to continue this effort and reuse my
   list.
