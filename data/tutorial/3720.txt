deap documentation[10]  

   deap is a novel evolutionary computation framework for rapid
   prototyping and testing of ideas. it seeks to make algorithms explicit
   and data structures transparent. it works in perfect harmony with
   parallelisation mechanism such as multiprocessing and [11]scoop. the
   following documentation presents the key concepts and many features to
   build your own evolutions.

   getting help

   having trouble? we   d like to help!
     * search for information in the archives of the [12]deap-users
       mailing list, or post a question.
     * report bugs with deap in our [13]issue tracker.
