   a large-scale video benchmark for human activity understanding

   our benchmark aims at covering a wide range of complex human activities
   that are of interest to people in their daily living. we illustrate
   three scenarios in which activitynet can be used to compare algorithms
   for human activity understanding: global video classification,trimmed
   activity classification and activity detection.
