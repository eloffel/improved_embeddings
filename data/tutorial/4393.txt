readme.md

pytorch examples

   a repository showcasing examples of using [136]pytorch
     * mnist convnets
     * word level id38 using lstm id56s
     * training id163 classifiers with residual networks
     * id3 (dcgan)
     * variational auto-encoders
     * superresolution using an efficient sub-pixel convolutional neural
       network
     * hogwild training of shared convnets across multiple processes on
       mnist
     * training a cartpole to balance in openai gym with actor-critic
     * natural language id136 (snli) with glove vectors, lstms, and
       torchtext
     * time sequence prediction - create an lstm to learn sine waves

   additionally, a list of good examples hosted in their own repositories:
     * [137]id4 using sequence-to-sequence id56 with
       attention (openid4)
