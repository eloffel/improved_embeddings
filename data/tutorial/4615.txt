deep learning from scratch: theory and implementation

   this is a multi-part series of tutorials, in which we develop the
   mathematical and algorithmic underpinnings of deep neural networks from
   scratch and implement our own neural network library in python,
   mimicing the [19]tensorflow api.

   start with the first part: [20]i: computational graphs.
