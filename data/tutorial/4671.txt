checking out id84 with id167

   [16]go to the profile of hannah yan han
   [17]hannah yan han (button) blockedunblock (button) followfollowing
   aug 30, 2017

   today i explored applying id167 on two high-dimension datasets: the
   classic mnist and the nouveau fashionmnist.

   you can read all about fashionmnist [18]here which is set out to be
   mnist scaled up in complexity.

   while mnist contains handwritten digits from 0 to 9, fashionmnist
   contains 10 different kinds of attires from t-shirts to dresses to
   trousers.

   i ran id167 on the entire original mnist training set, which is rather
   well-separated, and compared it with fashionmnist.
   [1*myrorvtduwus4rbgqxiiug.png]
   mnist in 2d

   and observed some overlapping in fashionmnist.
   [1*n1vzimgkhrmnwqfjmbgoaq.png]
   fashionmnist in 3d

   we can further rotate it in plotly and remove the clearly separately
   classes to identify the overlapping classes: t-shirt, shirt and coat.
