   published on sep 27, 2016

   the talks at the deep learning school on september 24/25, 2016 were
   amazing. i clipped out individual talks from the full live streams and
   provided links to each below in case that's useful for people who want
   to watch specific talks several times (like i do). please check out the
   official website ([18]http://www.bayareadlschool.org) and full live
   streams below.
   having read, watched, and presented deep learning material over the
   past few years, i have to say that this is one of the best collection
   of introductory deep learning talks i've yet encountered. here are
   links to the individual talks and the full live streams for the two
   days:
   1. foundations of deep learning (hugo larochelle, twitter) -
   [19]https://youtu.be/zij_ftbjhsk
   2. deep learning for id161 (andrej karpathy, openai) -
   [20]https://youtu.be/u6aeyuemt0m
   3. deep learning for natural language processing (richard socher,
   salesforce) - [21]https://youtu.be/ogk1v1jqitw
   4. tensorflow tutorial (sherry moore, google brain) -
   [22]https://youtu.be/ejec3id_h0w
   5. foundations of unsupervised deep learning (ruslan salakhutdinov,
   cmu) - [23]https://youtu.be/rk6bchqean8
   6. nuts and bolts of applying deep learning (andrew ng) -
   [24]https://youtu.be/f1ka6a13s9i
   7. deep id23 (john schulman, openai) -
   [25]https://youtu.be/ptaih9ksnjo
   8. theano tutorial (pascal lamblin, mila) -
   [26]https://youtu.be/ou8i1oj9hhi
   9. deep learning for id103 (adam coates, baidu) -
   [27]https://youtu.be/g-sndkf7mcs
   10. torch tutorial (alex wiltschko, twitter) -
   [28]https://youtu.be/l1shcj3qdnc
   11. sequence to sequence deep learning (quoc le, google) -
   [29]https://youtu.be/g5ry_sujih4
   12. foundations and challenges of deep learning (yoshua bengio) -
   [30]https://youtu.be/11rsu_wwztc
   full day live streams:
   day 1: [31]https://youtu.be/eyovmatoux0
   day 2: [32]https://youtu.be/9dxiaecyjry
   go to [33]http://www.bayareadlschool.org for more information on the
   event, speaker bios, slides, etc. huge thanks to the organizers (shubho
   sengupta et al) for making this event happen.
