welcome to foolbox[22]  

   foolbox is a python toolbox to create adversarial examples that fool
   neural networks.

   it comes with support for many frameworks to build models including
     * tensorflow
     * pytorch
     * theano
     * keras
     * lasagne
     * mxnet

   and it is easy to extend to other frameworks.

   in addition, it comes with a large collection of adversarial attacks,
   both gradient-based attacks as well as black-box attacks. see
   [23]foolbox.attacks for details.

   the source code and a [24]minimal working example can be found on
   [25]github.

robust vision benchmark[26]  

   [27]http://bethgelab.org/media/banners/benchmark_banner.png

   you might want to have a look at our recently announced [28]robust
   vision benchmark, a benchmark for adversarial attacks and the
   robustness of machine learning models.
