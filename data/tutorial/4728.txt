what we do

   at etymo, we build a light id13 layer on top of your
   databases, allowing you to extract value from your data

web api

   easy to use restful web api, get insights without complicated sql
   queries

secure

   added graph layer is access controlled, keeping your data safe and
   secure

id13

   we link your databases into a id13, allowing you to extract
   value from your data
