the only id203 cheatsheet you'll ever need

what is this?

   this is an 10-page id203 cheatsheet compiled from harvard's
   introduction to id203 course, taught by joe blitzstein
   ([14]@stat110). the id203 formula sheet summarizes important
   id203 id203 concepts, formulas, and distributions, with
   figures, examples, and stories.

who wrote this?

   joe blitzstein ([15]@stat110, [16]quora) - professor of statistics at
   harvard, and instructor of harvard's stat 110 (id203).

   william chen ([17]@wzchen, [18]quora) - a data scientist at quora and a
   former teaching fellow for harvard's stat 110 (id203).
   [19]download the id203 cheatsheet

get the code

   the latex file is available on github ([20]id203 cheatsheet
   latex). comments / pull requests / questions / raised issues are highly

get the book

   this cheatsheet is 100% based on the content in blitzstein's
   introduction to id203 course. get the [27]introduction to
   id203 (affilliate link) textbook from the course for the full
   treatment of the material.
   9781466575578.jpg 9781466575578.jpg

   the material in the cheatsheet is based tightly off the material in
   this textbook, [28]introduction to id203, by joe blitzstein and
   jessy hwang. i owe a lot of my own id203 understanding to the
   material in this textbook.
