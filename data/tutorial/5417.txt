allennlp

an open-source nlp research library, built on pytorch

   [11]view demo[12]get started
   allennlp is a free, open-source project from [13]ai2.

deep learning for nlp

   allennlp makes it easy to design and evaluate new deep learning models
   for nearly any nlp problem, along with the infrastructure to easily run
   them in the cloud or on your laptop.

   [14]get started

state of the art models

   allennlp includes reference implementations of high quality models for
   both core nlp problems (e.g. id14) and nlp
   applications (e.g. id123).

   [15]view models

proudly built by ai2

   allennlp is built and maintained by the allen institute for artificial
   intelligence, in close collaboration with researchers at the university
   of washington and elsewhere. with a dedicated team of best-in-field
   researchers and software engineers, the allennlp project is uniquely
   positioned for long-term growth alongside a vibrant open-source
   development community.
