   published on mar 7, 2015

   google deepmind created an artificial intelligence program using deep
   id23 that plays atari games and improves itself to a
   superhuman level. it is capable of playing many atari games and uses a
   combination of deep id158s and reinforcement
   learning. after presenting their initial results with the algorithm,
   google almost immediately acquired the company for several hundred
   million dollars, hence the name google deepmind. please enjoy the
   footage and let me know if you have any questions regarding deep
   learning!
