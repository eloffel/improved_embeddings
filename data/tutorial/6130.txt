readme.md

datasets for natural language processing

   this is a list of datasets/corpora for nlp tasks, in reverse
   chronological order. suggestions and pull requests are welcome. the
   goal is to make this a collaborative effort to maintain an updated list
   of quality datasets.

areas

     * [63]id53
     * [64]dialogue systems
     * [65]goal-oriented dialogue systems

id53

     * (ms marco) ms marco: a human generated machine reading
       comprehension dataset, 2016 [66][paper] [67][data]
     * (newsqa) newsqa: a machine comprehension dataset, 2016 [68][paper]
       [69][data]
     * (squad) squad: 100,000+ questions for machine comprehension of
       text, 2016 [70][paper] [71][data]
     * (graphquestions) on generating characteristic-rich question sets
       for qa evaluation, 2016 [72][paper] [73][data]
     * (story cloze) a corpus and cloze evaluation for deeper
       understanding of commonsense stories, 2016 [74][paper] [75][data]
     * (children's book test) the goldilocks principle: reading children's
       books with explicit memory representations, 2015 [76][paper]
       [77][data]
     * (simplequestions) large-scale simple id53 with memory
       networks, 2015 [78][paper] [79][data]
     * (wikiqa) wikiqa: a challenge dataset for open-domain question
       answering, 2015 [80][paper] [81][data]
     * (id98-dailymail) teaching machines to read and comprehend, 2015
       [82][paper] [83][code to generate] [84][data]
     * (quizbowl) a neural network for factoid id53 over
       paragraphs, 2014 [85][paper] [86][data]
     * (mctest) mctest: a challenge dataset for the open-domain machine
       comprehension of text, 2013 [87][paper] [88][data] [89][alternate
       data link]
     * (qasent) what is the jeopardy model? a quasisynchronous grammar for
       qa, 2007 [90][paper] [91][data]

dialogue systems

     * (ubuntu dialogue corpus) the ubuntu dialogue corpus : a large
       dataset for research in unstructured multi-turn dialogue systems,
       2015 [92][paper] [93][data]

goal-oriented dialogue systems

     * (frames) frames: a corpus for adding memory to goal-oriented
       dialogue systems, 2016 [94][paper] [95][data]
     * (dstc 2 & 3) dialog state tracking challenge 2 & 3, 2013
       [96][paper] [97][data]
