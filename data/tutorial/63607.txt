readme.md

pytorch-capsule

   a pytorch implementation of hinton's "dynamic routing between
   capsules".

   [68]https://arxiv.org/pdf/1710.09829.pdf

   thanks to @naturomics for his tensorflow implementation which was a
   useful guide and sanity check.
   [69]https://github.com/naturomics/capsnet-tensorflow

   to use:

   $ python main.py
