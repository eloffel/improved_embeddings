generative id53

   this repository contains information about downloading the corpus for
   generative id53. for a detailed description of the
   corpus, please read the following paper. please cite the paper if you
   use this corpus in your work.

   jun yin, xin jiang, zhengdong lu, lifeng shang, hang li, and xiaoming
   li. [61]neural generative id53. arxiv:1512.01337, 2015.

download corpus

   download the corpus at [62]http://61.93.89.94/cqa_kb_ch/ with
username: huawei
password: noah

   please refer to readme.txt in the folder for the explanation of the
   data format.

   please note that texts in the corpus are mostly in chinese.
