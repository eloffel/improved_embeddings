readme.md

cyborg writer

   [87]cyborg writer screencast

   this is an experimental text editor with an neural text synthesizer.

   feeling writer's block? hit and an id158 running in
   your browser will finish your sentence as if it were written by
   shakespeare, the us supreme court, or tupac shakur.

training

   interested in training your own corpus? check out the training/
   directory, which has instructions for running the training script on
   keras.

credits

   this project was inspired by robin sloan's id56-writer, and is powered
   by [88]tensorfire and [89]codemirror. it's built by [90]kevin kwok,
   guillermo webster, anish athalye, and logan engstrom.
