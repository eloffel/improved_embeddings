readme.md

convisualize

   visualizations for convolutional neural networks (id98s) in pytorch

   the corresponding article can be found [81]here!

requirements:

     * pytorch
     * torchvision
     * numpy
     * matplotlib
     * pillow

   note: in case you don't have a gpu, remove all instances of "cuda" and
   "cpu" from the notebook before running.
