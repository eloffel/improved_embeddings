readme.md

ai&dl knowledge base

   ai&dl knowledge base, in short aidl_kb, is a curated list that is
   related to the [78]ai&dl facebook group about artificial intelligence
   and deep learning.

   our goal is to provide knowledge base for all ai&dl members and public.

contents

     * [79]group etiquette (mandatory for new group members)
     * [80]how to learn & basics
     * where to learn
          + [81]prerequisites (math & programming)
          + [82]machine learning (ml)
          + [83]deep learning (dl)
          + [84]id161 (cv)
          + [85]natural language processing (nlp)
          + [86]speech (asr)
          + [87]psychology and neuroscience
     * [88]other lists and people to follow
     * [89]public datasets
     * [90]blogs
