deeplearn

   welcome to deeplearn. this repository contains implementation of
   following research papers on nlp, cv, ml, and deep learning. visit my
   blog for more details - [95]deeplearn
- latest update : added _deeplearn_utils modules. check the releases for descrip
tion of new features.

   [1] [96]correlation neural networks. cv, id21,
   representation learning. [97]blog-post || [98]code

   [2] [99]reasoning with neural tensor networks for knowledge base
   completion. nlp, ml. [100]blog-post || [101]code

   [3] [102]common representation learning using step-based correlation
   multi-modal id98. cv, id21, representation learning.
   [103]code

   [4] [104]abid98: attention-based convolutional neural network for
   modeling sentence pairs. nlp, deep learning, sentence matching.
   [105]code

   [5] [106]learning to rank short text pairs with convolutional deep
   neural networks. nlp, deep learning, cqa. [107]code

   [6] [108]combining neural, statistical and external features for fake
   news stance identification. nlp, ir, deep learning. [109]code

   [7] [110]wikiqa: a challenge dataset for open-domain question
   answering. nlp, deep learning, cqa. [111]code

   [8] [112]siamese recurrent architectures for learning sentence
   similarity. nlp, sentence similarity, deep learning. [113]code

   [9] [114]convolutional neural tensor network architecture for community
   id53. nlp, deep learning, cqa. [115]code

   [10] [116]map-reduce for machine learning on multicore. map-reduce,
   hadoop, ml. [117]code

   [11] [118]teaching machines to read and comprehend. nlp, deep learning.
   [119]code

   [12] [120]improved representation learning for question answer
   matching. nlp, deep learning, cqa. [121]code

   [13] [122]external features for community id53. nlp, deep
   learning, cqa. [123]code

   [14] [124]id46 and disambiguation in indian
   mixed-script. nlp, ir, ml. [125]blog-post || [126]code

   [15] [127]construction of a semi-automated model for faq retrieval via
   short message service. nlp, ir, ml. [128]code
