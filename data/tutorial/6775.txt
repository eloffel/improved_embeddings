readme.md

incremental-id97

   modify id97 such that it's possible to "condition" on existing
   embeddings for some words, and induce embeddings for new words.

usage:

./id97 -train testdemo.txt -output testdemo.oldmodel -size 200 -threads 12
./id97 -train new_data.txt -output testdemo.newmodel -size 200 -threads 12 -
fixed-embeddings testdemo.oldmodel
