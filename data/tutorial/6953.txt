   this introduces the "essence of id202" series, aimed at
   animating the geometric intuitions underlying many of the topics taught
   in a standard id202 course.
   error corrections:
   - at one point i mistakenly allude to calculators using the taylor
   expansion of sine for its computations, when in reality most use cordic
   (or something like it).
   - around 30 seconds in, there is a typo in how the determinant is
   written, which should be ad - bc
