   welcome to the [31]python graph gallery. this website displays hundreds
   of charts, always providing the reproducible [32]python code! it aims
   to showcase the awesome dataviz possibilities of python and to help you
   benefit it. feel free to [33]propose a chart or [34]report a bug. any
   feedback is highly welcome. get in touch with the gallery by following
   it on [35]twitter, [36]facebook, or by [37]subscribing to the blog.
   note that [38]this online course  is another good resource to learn
   dataviz with python.
