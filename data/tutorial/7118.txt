readme.md

nl2code

   a syntactic neural model for parsing natural language to executable
   code [86]paper.

dataset and trained models

   get serialized datasets and trained models from [87]here. put models/
   and data/ folders under the root directory of the project.

usage

   to train new model
. train.sh [hs|django]

   to use trained model for decoding test sets
. run_trained_model.sh [hs|django]

dependencies

     * theano
     * vprof
     * nltk 3.2.1
     * astor 0.6
