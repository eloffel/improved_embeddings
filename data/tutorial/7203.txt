     abstract: draft of textbook chapter on id4. a
     comprehensive treatment of the topic, ranging from introduction to
     neural networks, computation graphs, description of the currently
     dominant attentional sequence-to-sequence model, recent refinements,
     alternative architectures and challenges. written as chapter for the
     textbook id151. used in the jhu fall 2017
     class on machine translation.
