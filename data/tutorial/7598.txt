universal pos tags

   these tags mark the core part-of-speech categories. to distinguish
   additional lexical and grammatical properties of words, use the
   [3]universal features.
   open class words closed class words  other
   [4]adj           [5]adp             [6]punct
   [7]adv           [8]aux             [9]sym

   [13]noun         [14]det

   alphabetical listing
     * [21]adj: adjective
     * [22]adp: adposition
     * [23]adv: adverb
     * [24]aux: auxiliary
     * [25]cconj: coordinating conjunction

     * [28]noun: noun

     * [30]part: particle

     * [34]sconj: subordinating conjunction
